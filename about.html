About InkTees

InkTees originated in 2013, early concepts came about from University coursework submitted by the CEO George Russell. The concept was popular amongst students who developed an interest. It was later in businesses development when the COO Chris Harvey partnered up with George to design and promote the business. It has since become a genuine business and started to accumulate a larger following outside of the University.
