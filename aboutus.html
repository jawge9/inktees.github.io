About us

We deliver unique designs that are not available anywhere else!

InkTees originated in 2013, early concepts came about from University coursework submitted by the Founder George Russell. The concept was popular amongst students and developed a mass interest. It was later in businesses development when the COO Chris Harvey partnered up with George to develop the the brand further. It has since become a genuine brand and started to accumulate a larger following outside of the University.
